Whispers of Te Tatau o te Pō:

Description: The sky parts to reveal the path of those before us. In hues of green and violet, the southern lights guide our eyes upward — toward Matariki, and the stories written in the stars.

I’m an impressionistic painter and art therapist. My most inspiring journey and source of artistic energy is beautiful New Zealand. Through my work, I express bright energy, joy in the moment, and a sense of hope. Thank you for being here.
